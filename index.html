About

ACAC is dedicated to providing both educational and social activities to establish a community and raise awareness about multi-generational Asian Canadian and international experiences.

We hope to continue expanding our community and our reach to celebrate Asian Canadian identity. To accomplish our mission, we host events such as foodie nights, guest speakers, and movie screenings.

Membership Information

ACAC members get a variety of discounts with their Membership Cards at both events and local businesses in the city.

With a membership, you’ll save about $2-$5 per event. Memberships are sold for $10 at each chapter.

With a membership, you’ll also get voting privileges in each election held in March of each year for the executive team and be able to run for a position. Lastly, you’ll get emails sent to your inbox with updates on the club!

Membership money goes straight to club funds and helps us organize events, buy supplies and keep event costs low!

Events

The Carleton University team hosts a mix of on-campus and off-campus events, all a mix of social and educational activities. Our goal is to offer low or no-cost admission fees for students & members.

We offer monthly events plus our regular lunchies, where we meet on campus and lunch together!
